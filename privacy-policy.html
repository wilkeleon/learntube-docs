Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

1. Introduction

At LearnTube, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our browser extension and related services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you sign up using Google Sign-In, we collect:

  • Your Google account email
  • Display name
  • Profile picture
  • Google account ID

2.2 Usage Data

We collect information about how you use LearnTube, including:

  • Questions you create and save
  • Your quiz attempts and performance
  • Websites you enable/disable for quizzes
  • Quiz frequency preferences
  • Dark/light mode preferences

2.3 Subscription Data

If you subscribe to our premium features, we store:

  • Subscription status and type
  • Trial period information
  • Subscription start and end dates
  • Payment status (through Stripe)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and personalize our services
  • Generate personalized quizzes based on your settings
  • Track your learning progress and quiz performance
  • Manage your subscription and premium features
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Send important updates about your account or our services

4. Data Storage and Security

We store your data securely using Firebase's infrastructure. Your data is:

  • Encrypted during transmission using HTTPS
  • Protected by Google's security infrastructure
  • Accessible only to you and our authorized administrators
  • Backed up regularly to prevent data loss

Payment information is handled securely by Stripe and is never stored on our servers.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Export your data (available in Account Settings)
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection
  • Update your preferences and personal information

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • All your personal information is permanently deleted
  • Your questions and quiz attempts are removed
  • Your subscription data is deleted from our records
  • Anonymized usage statistics may be retained for analytics

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Authentication for sign-in
  • Firebase for data storage and authentication
  • Stripe for payment processing
  • Chrome Storage for extension preferences

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the extension or via email. Continued use of our services after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
